Michael Jordan

The best player in the NBA


Career Achievements

  • Born February 17, 1963
  • Played college basketball for three seasons under coach Dean Smith with the North Carolina Tar Heels
  • Joined the Bulls in 1984 as the third overall draft pick
  • Individual accolades and accomplishments include six NBA Finals Most Valuable Player (MVP) Awards
  • Fueled the success of Nike's Air Jordan sneakers
  • Became the first billionaire player in NBA history
